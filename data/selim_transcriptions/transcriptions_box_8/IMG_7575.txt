Sir, the Director of Works.

I have the honor to inform you that we approve the expenditure of L.E. 7 for the iron barrier to be installed in the tomb of Nakht, replacing the wooden barrier which was eaten by white ants (termites).

Please accept, Sir, the Director of Works, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.

For THE DIRECTOR GENERAL.
[Signature: H. Gauthier]

Monsieur E. Baraize,
Director of Works

Medinet Habu. LUXOR.
