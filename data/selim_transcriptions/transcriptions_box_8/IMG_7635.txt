[Stamp: A genuine lamp decorated with the star Hassan Nabulsi]

The noble Egyptian has inherited the well-known qualities of our excellent soap for decades, and the name (Hassan Haqiqi Faraj Hassan Nabulsi) is clear. We have worked with sincerity, perfection, and good craftsmanship. And with all due respect, we announce our gratitude for the public's demand for our soap, such a great demand that has caused imitators to adopt titles for their soap that resemble the title of our well-known soap. However, while it may be easy for them to use some words from our soap's brand, it is impossible for any imitator to adopt (Hassan Nabulsi) inside the image of the star under the crescent, as is our registered and stamped soap brand shown above. We hope that you will notice this on every piece of our soap so that any suspicion of using counterfeit, adulterated soap will be removed from your minds.

Please accept our respect,
Faraj Hassan Al-Nabulsi
Owner of soap factories

Siraj and Said Bazar'a
