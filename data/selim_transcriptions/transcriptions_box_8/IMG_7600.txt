Reference Number: N° 469
Document Date: 1926-04-28
Sender: P. Lacau, Director General of the Antiquities Service
Recipient: Monsieur E. Baraize, Directeur de Travaux
Excavation Site: Osireion at Abydos
Entities Mentioned: P. Lacau, C.L. Edgar, M. Baraize, Mohammed Eff. Ahmed Ibrahim, Egyptian Antiquities Service
Thematic Tags: Excavation Funding, Personnel Assignment, Abydos, Osireion
Brief Summary: This service order allocates L.E. 200 to Mr. Baraize for work at the Osireion in Abydos and assigns Mohammed Eff. Ahmed Ibrahim to the site. A copy of the order is transmitted to Mr. Baraize for his information and necessary action.
English Translation: Cairo, April 7, 1926.
[Handwritten Arabic: Department of Antiquities]

ANTIQUITIES SERVICE
---------
General Directorate

[Pencil note: 42.80/27]

Subject:
SERVICE ORDER N° 469

A sum of L.E. 200 is placed at the disposal of Mr. Baraize for the work at the Osireion in Abydos.
He will employ Mohammed Eff. Ahmed Ibrahim on this site.
Cairo, April 28, 1926.
THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,
signed: P. Lacau.
--------------------

Copy transmitted to Mr. E. Baraize, for information and necessary action.

For THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,
[Handwritten signature: C.L. Edgar]
