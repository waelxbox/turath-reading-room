Reference Number: N° 440
Document Date: 1925-09-03
Sender: C.C. Edgar, Directeur Général du Service des Antiquités
Recipient: Monsieur E. Baraize, Directeur de Travaux
Excavation Site: Giza (Sphinx, Mastabas)
Entities Mentioned: M. Baraize, MM. Reisner, Junker, Ahmed Eff. Choukri, C.C. Edgar, Hatem, Service des Antiquités
Thematic Tags: Conservation, Site Maintenance, Financial Allocation, Excavation Management
Brief Summary: This service order directs M. Baraize to oversee the repair of the Sphinx's neck and the removal of sand, as well as to examine and repair mastaba roofs in consultation with MM. Reisner and Junker, with an initial allocation of 1000 L.E. for these works.
English Translation: ANTIQUITIES SERVICE
Directorate General

SERVICE ORDER N° 440

Mr. Baraize is tasked with repairing the neck of the Sphinx and having the sand removed from in front of it. He will also examine, in agreement with Messrs. Reisner and Junker, the mastaba roofs to be repaired, and he will have this work carried out by Ahmed Eff. Choukri.

To begin, a sum of L.E.1000, to be taken from article 5a and then from the reserve, is placed at his disposal.

Cairo, September 3, 1925.
For THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,
signed: C.C. Edgar

--------------------

Copy transmitted to Mr. Baraize for all useful purposes.
For THE HEAD OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE SERVICE,
[Signature: Hatem]
