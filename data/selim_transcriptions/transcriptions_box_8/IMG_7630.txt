[Pencil note:] To re-address invoice Mr. Georges Chehata
[Pencil note:] To Mr. Ibrahim Abdel Gawad who is on leave. The invoice has not been paid.

This is to be returned to us from the Administration.
We inform your service with this letter about Mr. Georges Chehata's invoice, addressed to Ibrahim Abdel Gawad who is on leave. The invoice is 4554 EGP.
Due to the prohibition by [illegible name/phrase], the amount from this inspection report was disbursed via the National Bank.
And we inform you that we sent the description and the requested expense error. I hope you will commit to complying with this order by sending the mentioned amount intact directly to the bank by check on the National Bank as you indicate.

Please accept our highest regards.
