The esteemed Monsieur Emile Baraize, Director of Works of the Service,
In response to your letter No. 14 dated 1924/5/21, we inform you that you have clarified that your rank allows you to use a private vehicle at your disposal throughout the day for use at any time you wish. The Service does not object to this, and all we want to achieve is to reduce expenses in this regard to the lowest possible extent, in pursuit of economy. Therefore, we are looking for one of the vehicle owners who would accept to rent a vehicle to you for a specific monthly fee instead of paying 150 milliemes daily for transportation, so that the savings resulting from using the first method are not less than 500/50 of the total spent according to the current method.
And since your Excellency appreciates the current economic circumstances, we therefore request you to reconsider this matter and inform us.
Please accept our highest regards,
Deputy Director General
[Stamp: Egyptian Antiquities Service]
