Document Date: 1920-05-20
Sender: Director of Stores
Recipient: Head of the Deposits Section
Entities Mentioned: Ibrahim Rashid, National Bank
Thematic Tags: Financial Administration, Payment Processing, Inter-departmental Communication, Treasury Operations
Brief Summary: A memo from the Director of Stores to the Head of the Deposits Section, dated May 20, 1920, concerning the processing of a 250-pound payment via a National Bank check for fees, and requesting the amount be sent directly to the applicant. It also notes that employee Ibrahim Rashid received an item and requested further documents.
English Translation: I handed the check to the bank employee.
Oh, a bill for the amount of 250 pounds.
On 20/5/1920.
And returned it on the same day.

Stores Department
Reference
Number of attachments

In the name of God
To His Excellency, the Head of the Deposits Section,
We send to your esteemed self, attached herewith, a bill for the amount of 250 pounds,
To the bank employee for payment of fees and its return to the treasury.
Employee Ibrahim Rashid received it with the item and requested the rest of the documents.
Oh, the check, send the mentioned amount directly to the applicant,
By virtue of a check on the National Bank in Al-Sheikh.
Please accept our highest regards.
(Oh, Director of Stores)
