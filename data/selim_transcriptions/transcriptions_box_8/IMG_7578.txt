Cairo, February 1st, 1928.

Subject. [Handwritten Arabic: Very urgent / before sale / materials and wages]
[Pencil note: Urgent]

Mr. Director of Works,

Following the verbal discussion you had on January 28th last with Mr. the Director General in Luxor regarding the credits opened for the Abydos site, I have the honor to inform you that we note that purchases made in Cairo for your various sites should no longer be drawn from the credits allocated to your works. We are taking the necessary steps concerning the credits that have been granted to you for the Abydos site.

By your letter of January 24th last, No. 270, you requested to draw a sum of L.E.100 from the credit of L.E.200 allocated to the works at Deir el-Bahari. We kindly ask you to specify whether this latter sum should be added to the Abydos credits, or to those of the tomb of Ramose, as understood by Mr. the Director General during your last discussion in Luxor. Your aforementioned letter not being precise, I would be obliged if you would clarify this point for us.

Please accept, Mr. Director of Works, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.

FOR THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,
[Signature: H. Gauthier]
[Handwritten: E.Baraize,]
[Handwritten: Director of Works,]
[Handwritten: Service of Antiquities,]
[Handwritten: Baliah]

Monsieur le Directeur de Travaux,

Comme suite à l'entretien verbal que vous
avez eu le 28 Janvier dernier avec M. le Directeur
Général à Louxor au sujet des crédits ouverts
pour le chantier d'Abydos, j'ai l'honneur de vous

faits au Caire pour vos différents chantiers ne

Par votre lettre du 24 Janvier dernier, N°270,
vous avez demandé de prélever sur le crédit de

M. le Directeur Général au cours de votre dernier
entretien à Louxor. Votre lettre précitée n'étant
pas précise, je vous serais obligé de nous fixer
sur ce point.

Veuillez agréer, Monsieur le Directeur de
Travaux, l'assurance de ma considération dis-
tinguée.
