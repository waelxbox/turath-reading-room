GENERAL DIRECTION
OF THE
ANTIQUITIES SERVICE

(MODEL N° 40 — [Handwritten Arabic: اورنيك نمرة ٤٠])

Cairo, January 3, 1924.

N° 22-20/14
Attachments ______

Sir,

I have the honor to confirm my dispatch of today and to inform you that a supplementary credit of L.E. 400 is granted to you for the continuation of your work. I wish, however, to draw your attention to the fact that it would be desirable, in order to avoid any observation, that requests for supplementary credit be submitted to us at least 15 days before the complete exhaustion of the original credit.

Please accept, Sir, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.

[Handwritten Arabic: س.س]

THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,
[Signature: J.E. Quibell]

[Pencil note: Noted]
Monsieur E. Baraize,
Director of Works,
Antiquities Service,
[Stamp: CAIRO / JA 25 / 1924 / [Handwritten Arabic: القاهرة]]
