Attachments_

Cairo, November 15, 1921

SUBJECT.— Repairs to be carried out at the temple of Kom Ombo.

Mr. Director of Works,

In response to your letter of November 9th current, N°71, I have the honor to inform you that I approve your proposal to undertake the repairs to be carried out at the temple of Kom Ombo, upon your return from Philae.

Please accept, Mr. Director, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.

THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,
[Handwritten signature: P. Lacau]

Mr. E. Baraize,
Director of Works of the Antiquities Service,
at [Pencil note: Aswan] [Handwritten: Shellal].
