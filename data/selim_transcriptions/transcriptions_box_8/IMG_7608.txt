English Translation: --Antiquities Service--
--SERVICE ORDER N°387.--
--CREDIT--

For the 1923-1924 campaign, Mr. Baraize
will carry out the following works:
          1°..Clearing of the dependencies of the Temple
DENDERAH-- of Isis, avenue joining the gate of Isis.
          2°..Clearing of the small Mamisi of Nectanebo, study of the roof.
          3°..Deep soundings between the Temple and the basin.
                                                               Credit:L.Eg:
                                                               ========== 500.

          1°..Construction of the roof, with tubular bricks, of the hall of ten columns.
--ABYDOS-- 2°..Continuation of the clearing of the enclosure wall and dependencies.
                                                               Credit:L.Eg:
                                                               ========== 400

          1°..Repair of the foundations of the portico of Ramesses II in the great court.
--LUXOR-- 2°..Column to be restored on the West side of the great court. Repair of the "MARCHAND" architrave.
                                                               Credit:L.Eg:
                                                               ========== 350

                                                               Cairo, November 7, 1923.
