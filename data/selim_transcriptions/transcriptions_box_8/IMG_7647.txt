Reference Number: N° 5
Document Date: 1924-12-29
Sender: T. Bould, Inspector, Inspectorate of Upper Egypt
Recipient: Monsieur E. Baraize, Directeur de Travaux
Excavation Site: Tomb N° 23, Luxor
Entities Mentioned: Mr Davies, The Dir. Gen., T. Bould, Monsieur E. Baraize
Thematic Tags: Tomb repair, Antiquities administration, Luxor, Upper Egypt
Brief Summary: T. Bould relays the Director General's request to Monsieur Baraize for the prompt repair of Tomb N° 23 and asks him to investigate and discuss the matter.
English Translation: SERVICE OF ANTIQUITIES
INSPECTORATE OF UPPER EGYPT.
Luxor 29-12-1924
N° 5
Enclosures 1

Dear Monsieur Baraze,
The enclosed letter of Mr Davies was handed to me by The Dir. Gen. while I was in Cairo. He likes that the tomb N° 23 should be repaired as early as possible. Will you be so kind as to look into this matter & discuss it. Believe me, Dear Mon. Baraze, Yours Very Sincerely, T. Bould
