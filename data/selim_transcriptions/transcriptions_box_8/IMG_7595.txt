Reference Number: N° 490
Document Date: 1926-12-20
Sender: P. Lacau, Director General, Antiquities Service
Recipient: Monsieur E. Baraize, Directeur de Travaux, Abydos
Excavation Site: Abydos, Temple of Horemheb
Entities Mentioned: P. Lacau, M. Baraize, Service des Antiquités
Thematic Tags: Funding, Restoration, Excavation, Abydos
Brief Summary: This service order allocates 600 L.E. to Mr. Baraize for work at Abydos, with 400 L.E. designated for restoration of the back room and portico of the abattoir, and 200 L.E. for excavations on the south side and sanctuary of the Temple of Horemheb.
English Translation: ANTIQUITIES SERVICE
----------
General Directorate
----------

SERVICE ORDER N° 490

A sum of 600 L.E. is placed at the
disposal of Mr. Baraize for the works
at Abydos.

400 L.E. for restoration (back room and
portico of the abattoir)
Art. Restoration

200 L.E. for excavating the South side of the
temple and the location of the
sanctuary of the Temple of Horemheb.
Art. Excavations.

Cairo, December 20, 1926.

THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,
signed: P. Lacau
