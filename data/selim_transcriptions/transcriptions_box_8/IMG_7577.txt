No. 5-2/7
Subject: [Handwritten Arabic: Administrative order regarding land filling]

Attachments ______

Mr. Director of Works,

In response to your letter dated the 5th of this current March, N°323, I have the honor to inform you that I approve the estimate you have prepared for the provisional consolidation of the East part of the enclosure of the Temple of Luxor. We are placing at your disposal the sum of L.E.20 for this work, which must be executed urgently.

Please accept, Mr. Director of Works, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.

[Handwritten signature: H]
THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,

[Pencil note: Placa]

[Stamp: CAIRO MR 28.3.30P.]
[Stamp: (132 Q.) EGYP GOVERN المصرية]

Mr. E. Baraize,
Director of Works,
Antiquities Service,
at Luxor.
