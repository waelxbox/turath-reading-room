GENERAL DIRECTORATE.
_
N° 22.20/17
_
Enclosures _._

Subject :

Sir, Director of Works,

Following my letter of yesterday, N°22.20/17, I have the honor to inform you that we are placing at your disposal a sum of L.E.100 to enable you to complete the repair of the mastabas and to consolidate the retaining wall around the Sphinx.

Please accept, Sir, Director of Works, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.

[Handwritten initial: H]

THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,

[Handwritten signature: Placer]

Monsieur E. Baraize,
Director of Works
of the Antiquities Service,
Pyramids Post.
