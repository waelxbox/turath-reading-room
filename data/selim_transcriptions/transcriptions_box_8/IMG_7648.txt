[Handwritten Arabic: Memo regarding sebakh at Denderah, Luxor 1924 12/18 Memo regarding sebakh at Denderah, Luxor 1924 12/18]

Luxor 18-12-924

Dear Mr. Baraize
I would like to write to you in
French, but I am afraid that I make many
mistakes.
When I came back here yesterday, I
heard from the Service that the Sir, fr. has
charged you to verify the question of the
irregular way which was used in removing
Sebakh at Denderah. Please request my report
on the case, if it is not sent to you, & compare
it with the facts. I am sure that you would
never find any thing wrong in it.
Yesterday, on my way back, the Chief Saffi
confessed to me that he gave the Inspector some
Sebakh money which never had been registered to
service & that the Inspector said to him that
such money were saved for the Service.
Please Talk to the Chief Saffi privately
of the fact; & of what he said to me yesterday.
& please do not forget to ask him of what I
said to the Inspector in his presence while we
were together at Denderah.
All what I want is to assure to
