ANTIQUITIES SERVICE
GENERAL DIRECTORATE.
ALEXANDRIA INSPECTORATE

No. 5-25/9.

Attachments :-

Cairo,
Alexandria, October 18, 1931.

Sir, Director of Works,

I have the honor to inform you that a credit of L.E. 1900 has been placed at your disposal for the consolidation works of the monuments of Nubia. This credit is allocated as follows: L.E. 1500 for the consolidation of the platform located in front of the temple of Abu Simbel, and L.E. 400 for the consolidation of the lower foundations of the temples of Gartas, Dendour, Wadi es Seboua, and Moharraqa.

This credit must not, under any circumstances, be exceeded, and the final expenditure justification sheets must be submitted to us before the end of March 1932.

Please accept, Sir, Director of Works, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.

For THE DIRECTOR GENERAL.

[Handwritten Arabic: (500/1938)]

[Signature: A. Gauthier]
