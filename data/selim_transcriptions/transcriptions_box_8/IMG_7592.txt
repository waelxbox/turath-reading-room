Baraize that funds have been allocated for excavation work at Abydos, Deir el-Bahari, and Ramose, effective May 1st, 1927, to continue the current campaign, with purchases made in Cairo not to be charged against these credits.

ANTIQUITIES SERVICE. Cairo, April 28, 1927.

GENERAL DIRECTORATE. [Handwritten Arabic: 'S. W. - 2 - S. W.']

N° 22.20/17 Subject: [Handwritten Arabic: 'S. W.']

Attached documents

Mr. Director of Works,

Following my letter of the 21st instant,
N°22.20/17, I have the honor to inform you
that we are granting you the following credits,
effective from May 1st next, to enable you to
continue your work on the various sites
until the end of this campaign:
L.E. 270 for the Abydos site
L.E. 30 " " Deir el-Bahari site
L.E. 300 " " Ramose site.

It is understood that purchases made in
Cairo according to your orders will not be
charged against these credits.

Please accept, Mr. Director of
Works, the assurance of my distinguished
consideration.

THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,
[Stamp: EGYPT GOVERNMENT 152 D.]
[Handwritten Arabic: 'S. W.']

Mr. E. Baraize,
Director of Works,
Antiquities Service,
at Abydos, via Balianah
