Brief Summary: The Director General of the Egyptian Antiquities Service informs Monsieur Baraize of the approval to allocate 3000 Egyptian Pounds for various restoration works at several archaeological sites during the 1924-1925 work season, in response to Baraize's letter of November 23rd.

[Handwritten Arabic: Subject:
Approval to open the required credits for restoration works to be completed during the 1924-1925 work season.

The Honorable Monsieur Baraize, Director of Works of the Service in Qena,
In response to your French letter dated November 23rd of the current month, we reply that we have approved the opening of the required credits for carrying out restoration works in the places indicated below, during the work season spanning the years 1924-1925:
Pounds
1200 for restoration works in Dendera
1000 for restoration works in the Buried Serapeum
300 for restoration works in Luxor
250 for restoration works in Deir el-Bahari
250 for restoration works in the Temple of Mentuhotep
3000 Total credits opened

Therefore, it was necessary to write this for your information. 4
Written on November 27, 1924.]

[Handwritten Arabic: The Director General]
[Signature: J.E. Quibell]
[Handwritten Arabic: Noted by him]
