ANTIQUITIES SERVICE.
Cairo, May 25, 1922
GENERAL DIRECTION.

N° 27.2/14
Subject: Statues destined for the New York Museum.
Attached documents: I

Mr. Director of Works,

I have the honor to transmit to you herewith
the blue bulletin requested by your letter
of May 24th instant, (Registration N° 413) for
the railway transport of the two statues
destined for the New York Museum.

Please accept, Mr. Director,
the assurance of my distinguished consideration.

THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,
[Handwritten signature: Musan]

[Stamp: EGYF GOVER]
[Pencil note: 132 D.]
[Handwritten Arabic: مصر]
[Handwritten Arabic: 22]
[Stamp: CAIRO M 25 IV 22]
