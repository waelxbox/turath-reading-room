Reference Number: N° 424
Document Date: 1925-03-04
Sender: P. Lacau, Director General of the Antiquities Service
Recipient: Monsieur E. Baraize, Directeur de Travaux
Excavation Site: Medamoud, Médinet Habou
Entities Mentioned: M. Baraize, M. Breasted, P. Lacau
Thematic Tags: Excavation funding, Site management, Construction, Archaeological work scheduling
Brief Summary: This service order from the Director General allocates L.E. 60 for the construction of a storehouse at Medamoud and confirms a L.E. 500 credit for work at Medinet Habu, instructing that work should resume when M. Baraize is available and in consultation with M. Breasted.
English Translation: SERVICE ORDER N°387.

CREDIT-

ANTIQUITIES SERVICE
General Directorate

SERVICE ORDER N°.[Pencil note: 424]

1°.- Construct a storehouse for the sculpture fragments at Medamoud. Include a room for the ghaffir (watchman).

Credit ..... L.E. 60

2°.- For Medinet Habu, maintain the credit of L.E. 500 (letter of 11 February 1925, N°22.20/17) instead of L.E. 200 (service order N°422).

Resume work when M. Baraize is freer; consult with M. Breasted to inconvenience him as little as possible.

Cairo, 4 March 1925.

THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,

[Handwritten Arabic: س]
[Handwritten Arabic: س]

[Pencil note: 4.22-20/17] Copy transmitted to M. Baraize for necessary action.

THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,
[Handwritten Arabic: علم]
[Handwritten signature: Placau]
