ANTIQUITIES SERVICE.                                   Cairo, October 1, 1927.

GENERAL DIRECTORATE.

No. 4-7/65                                              Subject:
-----

Attachments
-----

Sir, Director of Works,

In response to your letter dated September 27th last, No. 113, I have the honor to inform you that we are placing at your disposal a sum of L.E. 350 for the work required for the transport to the Cairo Museum of the sarcophagus of Kha-Our discovered in 1926 at the Giza Pyramids.

Please accept, Sir, Director of Works, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.
[Handwritten mark: stylized initial/flourish]

                                                        For THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,
                                                        [Signature: A. Gauthier]

Mr. E. Baraize,
Director of Works,
Antiquities Service,
Cairo.
