Cairo, November 15, 1921

SUBJECT.- Work to be carried out at the Temple of Luxor.

Mr. Director of Works,

In response to your letter of November 9th current, Nº73, I have the honor to inform you that I approve the expenditure foreseen therein for the work to be carried out in the Court of Ramses II.
You may undertake this work as soon as you return from Philae or after Dendera, at your choice.
Please accept, Mr. Director, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.

THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,
[Signature: Placar]

Mr. E. Baraize,
Director of Works of the Antiquities Service,
at Assouan Shellal.
