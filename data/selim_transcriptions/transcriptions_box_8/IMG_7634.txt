Subject: Regarding the transport of the sarcophagus discovered at the Pyramids and the restoration of the supervising mastabas.

To His Excellency Monsieur Emile Baraize, Director of Works for the Service,
Further to your statement dated 1931/5/4
Regarding the request to open a credit of 350 Egyptian Pounds for the transport of the sarcophagus discovered
at the Pyramids by the University, and the restoration of the supervising mastabas discovered
also by the University, we kindly request that when sending the disbursement documents,
you specify the amount spent on the transport of the sarcophagus and also the amount spent
on the restoration of the mastabas, each type separately.
Please accept our highest regards,
Cairo on 1931/5/16
