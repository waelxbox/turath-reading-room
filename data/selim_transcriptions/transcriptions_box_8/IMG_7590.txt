Sir, Director of Works,

I thank you for the information you gave me about your two foundation deposits. These are isolated deposits; there were certainly several others in certain particularly important points of the construction, for example, the corners, under the doorposts, etc. It is unnecessary to search for them systematically this year, but if your trenches approach some of these points and you find other deposits, please carefully annotate the respective position of all objects; photograph them in situ if possible. Make a complete inventory of each deposit: all pottery from the deposit, even coarse ware, is of great importance as a type of vase and in terms of the number of examples.

I am telling M. Gauthier to credit you with an additional L.E. 200 for Abydos (including the price of the photographic equipment) if you deem it necessary, [Pencil note: Otherwise, I will pass by] to see these days. I will be at Abydos around the 18th or 20th of this February.

Please accept, Sir, Director of Works, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.

THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,
