ANTIQUITIES SERVICE.
GENERAL DIRECTION.

N°5.3/4

Attached documents 2

R.D.

Cairo, March 29, 1922

SUBJECT.- Works to be carried out at Saqqara.

Mr. Director of Works,

I have the honor to transmit herewith, in original, two letters N°384 and 404, from Mr. C. Firth, dated respectively the 15th and 24th of this month, concerning various works to be carried out at Saqqara.
I would be obliged if you would examine the works in question and prepare the estimates for them, either in May or next November, upon your return from leave.

Please accept, Mr. Director, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.

THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,

[Handwritten: Lacau]

OBJET.- Travaux à exécuter à Sakkara.

Monsieur le Directeur de Travaux,

en original, deux lettres N°384 et 404, de M.C. Firth,

soit en Novembre prochain, à votre retour de congé.

LE DIRECTEUR GENERAL,

[Handwritten: Lacau]
