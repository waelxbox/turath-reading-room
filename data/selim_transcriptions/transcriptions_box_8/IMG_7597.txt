ANTIQUITIES SERVICE.
—
GENERAL DIRECTION.
—
N° 22.20/17

Cairo, November 21, 1926.
Subject: [Knowledge of the existence of 50 pounds for labor to find a stored roof]

Attachments [Pencil note: 15.]

Sir,

In response to your letter dated November 18th instant, I have the honor to inform you that we are placing at your disposal a sum of L.E. 150 for the consolidation work at Mastaba el-Faraône. This sum will be drawn from the Saqqarah credit.

Please accept, Sir, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.

for THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,

[Handwritten signature: C.C. Edgar]

Mr. E. Baraize,
Director of Works,
Antiquities Service,
Pyramids Post
