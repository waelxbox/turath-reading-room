Mr. Director of Works,

In response to your letter dated April 11th current, N°13, I have the honor to inform you that we approve the transfer to the credit of Abydos of the L.E.20 previously granted for the works of Ramosé.

Please accept, Mr. Director of Works, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.

THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,
[Signature: Lacau]

Mr. Emile Baraize,
Director of Works,
Antiquities Service,
at Abydos, BALIANAH
