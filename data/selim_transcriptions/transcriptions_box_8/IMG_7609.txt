English Translation: NOTE TO M. BARAIZE ON THE WORKS TO BE CARRIED OUT AT ABYDOS IN 1922-1923.-
-----------
1°.- Covering of the three chapels to the right of the Great Pillared Hall which forms the back (west) of the temple. Study of the lighting of the bas-reliefs through this covering.

2°.- Emptying the doorless room (two stories) which forms the North-West corner of the temple. Exploring the floor which has never been examined.

3°.- Clearing the debris from the columned hall (overturned columns) which is next to the abattoir. Do not move the collapsed column drums until my arrival. Carefully sort all scattered stone blocks; there must be some that come from the cornice of the Hall.
