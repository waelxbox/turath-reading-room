Mr. Director of Works,

In response to your letter dated May 13th current, N°65, I have the honor to inform you that we would like to entrust you with the following works next October:
1) removal of the sarcophagus of Kha-our;
2) clearing in front of the Sphinx;
3) covering, consolidation and closing of different tombs on the plateau, among others in the concession of Mr. Junker.

I ask you to please review all of this when you pass through on your return, so that a cost estimate for the execution of these works can be established.

For the construction of the new Pyramids road, we must wait for the budget to be voted by Parliament.

Please accept, Mr. Director of Works, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.

THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,

[Handwritten signature: P. Lacau]

E. Baraize,
Director of Works,
Service of Antiquities,
Abydos, via Balianah.
