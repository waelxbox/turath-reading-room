Egyptian Antiquities Service
Administrative Order No. (517)

After reviewing the credit opened in the year 1937-1938 for the necessary restoration works for the Temple of Abydos, and based on what was presented to us by His Excellency the Head of the Administration Department, we have decided the following: -
Firstly - An amount of 100 (one hundred) pounds is to be added to this credit, bringing it to 440 pounds.
Secondly - The Accounts Department must observe this addition and record it in the relevant register, with informing His Excellency Monsieur Emile Baraize, Director of Works, of this.
Written on 1938/1/7

The Director General
Signed: P. Lacau

Sent to His Excellency Monsieur Emile Baraize

Cairo on 1938/1/8

The Director General
