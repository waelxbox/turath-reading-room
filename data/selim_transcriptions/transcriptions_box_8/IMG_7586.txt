ANTIQUITIES SERVICE.

GENERAL DIRECTORATE.

No. 22.20/17 Subject:

Cairo, [Handwritten: September 29] 1927.
[Handwritten: October 1]

Enclosures 1

Sir, Director of Works,

In response to your letter dated September 27th instant, No. 114, I have the honor to enclose herewith a statement providing details of the credits granted for the works on the tomb of Ramose at Gournah.

Please accept, Sir, Director of Works, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.

[Pencil note: RM]

For THE DIRECTOR GENERAL,
[Signature: H. Gauthier]

[Stamp: EGYPTIAN GOVERNMENT. [Handwritten Arabic: 10/12/1927]]

Monsieur E. Baraize,
Director of Works,
Antiquities Service,
Cairo.
