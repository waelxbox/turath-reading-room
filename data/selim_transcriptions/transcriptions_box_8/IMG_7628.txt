Listing requested and urgent

To the Honorable Assistant Director of Excavations, Antiquities Service, Giza
We send to your esteemed self with this letter an invoice for Ibrahim Ahmed El-Diwani for the amount of 425 and 4 [currency units] as the price of wood supplied for excavation works in the Giza Pyramids area. Please endorse and sign the invoice to confirm receipt of the item and its conformity to what was requested, then return it to us again - accompanied by the necessary inspection report on form 194 'Ain' - so that the necessary action can be taken regarding it and its value disbursed.

Please accept our highest regards,
