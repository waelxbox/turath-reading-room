I have the honor to inform you that the Board of Directors, in its session of February 27, 1923, decided to distribute the responsibilities of the Inspectors of Upper Egypt as follows:

1° The Chief Inspector will direct the work of the Upper Egypt Inspection and will be responsible for the work of the Assiout and Luxor Inspections.

2° The 2nd Class Inspector will direct the work of the Minieh and Middle Egypt Inspections and will be responsible for the work of the Beni-Souef and Fayoum Inspections.

3° The 3rd Class Inspector will direct the work of the Girgeh, Edfou, Esneh, Edfou, Aswan, and Lower Nubia Inspections and will be responsible for the work of the Kom-Ombo and Denderah Inspections.

4° The 4th Class Inspector will direct the work of the Qena, Nag-Hammadi, Abydos, and Middle Egypt Inspections and will be responsible for the work of the Beni-Souef and Fayoum Inspections.

5° The 5th Class Inspector will direct the work of the Inspections [text cut off].

J'ai l'honneur de vous informer que le Conseil d'Administration, dans sa séance du 27 Février 1923, a décidé de répartir les attributions des Inspecteurs de la Haute-Egypte comme suit:

1° L'Inspecteur en Chef dirigera les travaux de l'Inspection de la Haute-Egypte et sera chargé des travaux de l'Inspection d'Assiout et de Louxor.

2° L'Inspecteur de 2ème classe dirigera les travaux des Inspections de Minieh et de la Moyenne-Egypte et sera chargé des travaux de l'Inspection de Beni-Souef et de Fayoum.
