SERVICE ORDER for M. BARAIZE. N° (648)
==================================

M. Baraize will carry out the following works during this winter. Credits allocated to each work:

1°- AT LUXOR.
- Luxor Temple and restoration of 2 architraves £ 300
- Horemheb's Tomb, preparation, landings, stairs etc.................................... £ 300 [Pencil note: this, 1930]
- Deir el Bahari - pillar and one of the lower northern porticoes................................ £ 500
- Straighten the stele of Amenophis III ............ £ 400.
------------------------------------
£1500.

2°- ABYDOS.- Repair of the roof of the hypostyle hall ..... £ 600.
3°- SPHINX.- Clearing of the temple up to the median axis and survey of the plan...................................... £ 800. [Pencil note: mbr iné t-]
4°- MIT-REHINEH.- Repair of the walls surrounding the colossus and room for the throne as a store....................... £ 500.
5°- ASSOUAN.- Consolidations in the Amba Simeon monastery....... £ 300.
------------------------------------ [Pencil note: r]
TOTAL. £3700.

Cairo, 10 November 1930.
[Pencil note: w.L.]
THE DIRECTOR GENERAL.
[Handwritten signature: P. Lacau]
